Personal SWOT analysis

Strengths

 (What do you do best? What are your positive traits?)

Eager to learn whatever comes my way and hardworking.

Opportunities

 (How can you turn your strengths / weaknesses into opportunities?)

My eagerness, curiosity, and hardworking personality doesn't let me give up until I find answers to all my questions. Whereas my weakness of not being able to say No, makes me push myself beyond limits therefore, I learn & grow more.

Weaknesses

 (What personality traits may be holding you back in your studies / career? )

It's hard for me to say No. Sometimes I have to rearrange my list of priorities due to that but, I try my best to get everything on the list done within the time limit.

Threats

 (What obstacles do you face to succeed as a student? Could any of your weaknesses prevent you from succeeding in your studies / career?)

I do not see my weakness turning into any sort of threats. I would pay extra attention and do all my best to turn my weakness into my strength.
