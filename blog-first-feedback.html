First Feedback

Welcome Fatimah Abbasi,

Thank you for submitting your “Who Am I?” form. The feedback given here is for you to use when you start the program, so you do not have to respond to it just now. Your motivation seems to be a bit too general, what is the future profession youre interested in? Why do you want to study different programming languages? Why is technology interesting to you? How has research led you to choose this program? What are job opportunities that will be nice for you? I recommend you to search for the true motivation that will keep you going throughout the entire study program of 4 years. This will be very helpful in difficult situations or challenges that you may encounter during your education.

It is really nice to see youre interested in becoming a software engineer, why you believe that by becoming a software engineer you will “get to play your role”? what is your role in your eyes? Why? same as for your motivation try and search a bit more about this profession and find why is that matching you? Are there any other options you would like to consider? Why or why not?

I recommend you to participate in the ICT conference upcoming year where youll meet companies and can get more information about this profession and how it is to work as a software engineer.

Reading your SWOT analysis, it is really good to see you acknowledge and are aware of some of your strengths, being eager to learn and hardworking are very nice and valuable traits that will help you greatly during the education. Regarding your weakness described here, I am happy to see that you managed to see the opportunity in it too, as it can be useful and help you be determined. I do however recommend you to ask yourself when there are a lot of tasks or things to do, what is most important to you, what are your biggest priorities and why, then, according to that divide your tasks and time. Always remember to keep time for yourself and to rest as well so you will not feel overwhelmed during your studies.

I am looking forward to meeting you upcoming year and wish you a lovely summer break!
