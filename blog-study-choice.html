Study Choice

In today's hyper-connected world, technology is at the heart of nearly every aspect of our lives. From business and healthcare to education and entertainment, information and communication technology (ICT) underpins it all. This digital age presents a compelling case for pursuing a bachelor's degree in ICT, offering a pathway to a rewarding, future-proof career. In this article, we'll explore the reason why studying ICT at the undergraduate level is a smart choice for me.

Looking at the ICT course on the official HZ website, I knew that this was what I wanted. What caught my eye was, at the end of the study, I would be familiar with different programming languages at the highest level and the latest technologies. I was looking for a course that could help me achieve my desire to get better and better at something that matters to me, something that fascinates me and helps me find answers to all my questions and that can feed my curiosity. I felt like it was just RIGHT for me. My interest in AI has always made me want to achieve something in that specific field. While researching the field I realized not a lot of research had been done in this field compared to other science fields such as Nanobiology, Astronomy, or Quantum physics. The reason was not just that, it's a new field but also the fear of robots taking over in the human brain which hinders the research in this field. Imagine one fine day you wake up and you realize the world is now ruled by robots. Scary, right? Identified the problem, and now it's time to find a solution, and I will figure it out.

I researched, read, and thought, and then I had a hypothesis. The hypothesis starts “A boundary can be set through programming and coding that can let the AI do its job freely but they'll not be able to take over the humans." It's just like the Great Wall of China. Like how kings back in time used to build strong walls around their kingdoms to protect them from enemies. The same concept is thought to be applied here. A strong boundary would be built around AI, where it'll not be allowed to intervene and take over humans. Within the boundaries, the AI will be free to perform its task. Unfortunately, I have no prior coding experience but you don't need to have any coding experience to be a part of the ICT department here at HZ.

Now how is coding going to help us with it? Coding is through which we make the wall. For example, you need concrete, cement, and bricks to make a wall the same way we need coding to build this boundary.

Well, that is just a thought, a simple hypothesis. A hypothesis can be wrong, but that's not something that should stop you. It should be the driving force to keep you moving on. A new hypothesis means a new observation and a new conclusion which therefore means new and concrete data, knowledge, and information. All these take you a step closer to your destination. They are like a key that helps you open a new door to discovery and invention but sometimes you might have to take a U-turn, hence you learn more. Even if I'm not able to make the boundary, I'm sure I would have made sufficient discoveries and research in the field that can help someone else working in the field. Imagine every time someone talks about AI the first name that comes to their mind is Fatimah Abbasi. Just like when you talk about Gravity; Albert Einstein typically comes to your mind, Telephone; Alexander Graham Bell, Apple; Steve Jobs, Microsoft; and Bill Gates, and this will be my driving force throughout my study at HZ.

Studying ICT for a bachelor's degree is not just about learning technical skills; it's about preparing for a dynamic, technology-driven future. With high demand, versatility, innovation opportunities, and global impact, ICT offers a multitude of compelling reasons to embark on this educational journey. It is a field that combines my passion for AI, and technology with career prospects that are both exciting and rewarding, considering that I enrolled in an ICT program, and becoming a part of the digital revolution. My future in the world of ICT, and AI awaits me, and the possibilities are endless!
